Aura, the Clockwork Daughter
Origin: Created by Míriel in the East.

Visuals: She is breathtaking. Made of polished white porcelain and weir-wood, with joints of gold. She moves with an uncanny, ticking grace. She doesn't sleep, so she has spent the last 50 years dusting every single book in Elrond’s library.

Her Tragedy: She has absolute Knowledge but zero Connection.

She has memorized every book on healing. She knows the chemical composition of Athelas. She knows the songs.

But when she tries to pick up Vilya (The Ring of Air/Healing), it burns her. It rejects her artificial soul. She cannot cast the spells; she can only recite them.
